Power Saver Options

Firefox Native Throttling Settings

For maximum power savings, adjust these settings in about:config:

dom.timeout.background_budget_regeneration_rate
Default: 15000 (15 seconds)
Recommended for power saving: 30000 (30 seconds)

Controls how quickly background tabs regain their timer budget.

dom.timeout.background_throttling_max_budget
Default: 15000 (15 seconds)
Recommended for power saving: 5000 (5 seconds)

Sets maximum timer budget background tabs can accumulate.

dom.timeout.budget_throttling_max_delay
Default: 15000 (15 seconds)
Recommended for power saving: 5000 (5 seconds)

Determines maximum delay for timers in background tabs.

⚠️
Important Configuration Note

These settings require manual configuration in Firefox's advanced settings.

1 Click in your Firefox address bar
2 Type about:config and press Enter
3 Click "Accept the Risk and Continue" on the warning page
4 Search for the setting name and double-click to modify it

Warning: Changing advanced configuration preferences can impact Firefox performance or security. Always create a backup of your profile before making changes to about:config.
